Connect Four

(with Smart AI)

Connect four discs of the same color together either horizontally/vertically/diagonally to win. Play with another person or against an smart AI. Click on an empty hole to put your disc.

Thank you for playing the game! Click on the restart button after this if you wish to play again.

For best experience please use Google Chrome. This game is unable to work in Firefox at creation(15 Jan 2018). Testing.
